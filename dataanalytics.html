NYC Sanitation Waste Analysis: Program Effectiveness

A deep dive into public data using **PostgreSQL** and **Excel** to measure the efficiency of New York City's recycling classification program.

Platform: Data Analysis & SQL

Key Findings: Paper Waste Contribution by Borough

GOAL

To analyze public NYC Sanitation data to assess the effectiveness of the **refuse vs. recyclable classification program** across the five boroughs, identifying correct categorization, program success, or significant inefficiencies.

RESULT

Provided key **strategic findings** and actionable next steps in a final presentation (PPT/Slides format), allowing stakeholders to quickly evaluate program efficiency and areas needing policy revision.

PROJECT WORKFLOW

This included the full analytical workflow: **Data cleaning in Excel**, complex **querying via PostgreSQL** to extract insights, and **synthesis into the final presentation deliverable**.
