My Services

Whether you’re just starting to track your business data or ready to automate your reports, I offer flexible services that meet you where you are.

Data Cleaning & Setup

I transform messy spreadsheets and raw files into structured, reliable datasets ready for analysis. Get rid of errors and inconsistencies for good. Tools I Use: Excel, SQL, Python.

Dashboard Creation

I create visual dashboards that give you a complete, intuitive picture of your sales, finances, and performance. Using Excel, Power BI, or Looker Studio, I design dashboards that are easy to read, actionable, and tailored to your business needs. Tools I Use: Excel, Power BI, Looker Studio, Tableau.

Report Automation

I reduce manual work and save hours every month by automating recurring Excel or Power BI reports using Power Query or simple scripts, so you can focus on making decisions instead of compiling data. Tools I Use: Excel (Power Query, VBA) | Power BI | Python.

Data Consultation

I offer one-on-one sessions to understand your business pain points and review your data workflows — how data is collected, stored, and processed across spreadsheets, systems, or websites. Using a structured methodology, I identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies, and provide a short, actionable report with recommendations for better data flow, process improvements, and tools or automation (e.g., Excel, Power BI, Python, CRM integrations) to make your operations more efficient and insights easier to access. Methodology: Discovery & Assessment | Data Workflow Review | Bottleneck Analysis | Recommendations & Reporting.
