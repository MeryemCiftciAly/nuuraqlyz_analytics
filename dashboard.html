Expense Claims Overview: Strategic Spending Insights

GOAL

To demonstrate that an everyday task—the expense report—can be leveraged using Python cleaning and Tableau to generate strategic, actionable business decisions.

RESULT

Streamlined expense auditing, highlighting 'Rejected' vs. 'Approved' totals and specific trend shifts over time to inform policy adjustments.

PROJECT DURATION

2 Weeks. This comprehensive timeline covered the entire analytical lifecycle: initial data review and Python cleaning, data modeling, dashboard design, internal testing and troubleshooting, and final publication.

Re-Offense Program Analysis Dashboard

GOAL

To analyze data from a reform program to find insights behind the likelihood of re-offense by participants, assessing the effectiveness of interventions like education, drug abuse counseling, and anger management.

RESULT

Enabled immediate program assessment and allowed staff to identify high-risk participants based on age, education, and incident type frequency.

PROJECT DURATION

2 Weeks. This included the full lifecycle: data integration and cleaning, defining DAX data models, dashboard design and visualization, rigorous testing and quality assurance, iterative updates, and final deployment to the Power BI service.
